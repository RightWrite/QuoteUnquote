Privacy Policy

The Quote, Unquote application is Open-source software: it is provided at no cost, and generates no income.

It is an educational "Fair Use" application.

This page is meant to inform you of the policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal Information if you decide to use the application.

Information Collection and Use

The application is split into two Variants:

  • a Google Play Store variant
  • a F-Droid variant

The Google Play Store variant uses the following analytics platforms:

  • Google Play Services
  • Microsoft Visual Studio App Center

The F-Droid variant does not use any analytics.

Apart from these platforms, the application does not use any other service that may collect information about you.

Any information that is collected in analytics is anonymous.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may, though is unlikely to, be updated from time to time.
